About Us

We provide a token based economy for carbon trading using blockchain as a means of ensuring decentralization and transparency at a global level. We offer a platform for digital tracking of carbon emission permits that provides a safeguard against the hoarding of carbon credits. Our blockchain based solution, is an alternative to the already existing permit trading platforms used for buying and selling of different types of carbon credits. Hence, we aim at providing authenticity and transparency to the carbon credits exchange process through our solution.
