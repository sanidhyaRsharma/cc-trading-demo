General FAQ

Is Registration required?

Yes you need to register yourself in order to begin carbon trading. You can register yourself by going to this link

How can i generate the Carbon Credits?

You can generate Carbon Credit by selecting the GenerateCC option from the dropdown list which appears when you click on your profile. And filling all the necessary details about your project.You can directly go to the page using this link. You can also view your CC balance which appears right next to your profile.

How can i buy Carbon Credits?

You can buy carbon credits by going on to buy page by selecting the buy option from dropdown. You get a list of available carbon credits, you can submit a request to the owner of the CC for the purchase, the status of the request can be viewed in the your notification tab

How can i check my previous transactions? Can i view other transactions as well?

Transparency is one of the main goals of this platform.You can view all the transactions involving you by selecting the history option in from the dropdown. You can also view the records of all the trasactions happened so far by going to this link

How can a know who is buying the CC generated by me? Can i choose to whom i want to sell my CC?

Yes, you have the control to whom you want to sell the CC the buyer sends a request to you for the purchase it is up to you do you want to accept it or not
