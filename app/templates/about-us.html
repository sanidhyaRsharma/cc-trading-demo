About CCX

What is our platform about ?

Our platform offers a token based economy for carbon trading using blockchain as a means of ensuring decentralization and transparency at a global level. We offer a platform for digital tracking of carbon emission permits that provides a safeguard against the hoarding of carbon credits. Our blockchain based solution, is an alternative to the already existing permit trading platforms used for buying and selling of different types of carbon credits. Hence, we aim at providing authenticity and transparency to the carbon credits exchange process through our solution.

What we offer


TRANSPARENCY

The Carbon Credits exchange process in the existing scenario is exposed to numerous fraudulent activities which restrict the industries to participate in the exchange network. However our platform offers a blockchain based solution, which ensures that transparency is maintained at every step in the entire carbon credits exchange process. The salient features of blockchain such as immutability and authentic verification and validation of credits further attributes towards the transparency in the system.

TRUST

The trading of carbon credits involves multiple distrusting parties,therefore our platform acts as a blockchain intermediary to infuse trust into the exchange network system between multiple parties. A trusted platform like ours will foster participation from all over the world due to the improved reputation of the participating members thereby giving them incentives to be a part of the network.

REDUCED COMPLEXITY

Having an intermediary platform reduces the complexity of the carbon credits exchange process between the parties. Hence, the digitization of carbon credits and their online trading makes the entire process economical for the participants. We also provide the means to verify the authenticity with the help of digital signature on the certicate attached to the token issued thereby reducing any complications on the participant side.
